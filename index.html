About Me

I'm an applied mathematician working in Universidad Autonoma de Yucatan. I enjoy solving applied problems, and learning programming languages, as you can see from my Github frontpage. I collaborate actively with the physics olympiad trainers in my home country.

In what I am now

My interests in science and technology are very wide, as you can see from my current interests:

  • I'm helping to develop better policies for Abstraction & application, an electronic journal on applied maths. Currently, that means learning Open Journal System.
  • Richard Feynman is my hero, so I'm working on quantum walks with bosons, and hopefully next year will be working in entangled particles traversing a quantum network.
  • As I can't be very far from programming, I have developed a few simple web apps with javascript: from a personal trainer for the master of science entrance examination in the school where I work to a Birthday countdown.
